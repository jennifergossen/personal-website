About me

The story so far

I grew up in the south of Germany. As a first-generation academic, I relied on scholarships, curiosity, and a stubborn belief that unconventional paths can lead somewhere extraordinary.

I studied Corporate Management & Economics at Zeppelin Universität, spent a semester abroad at UC Berkeley, and worked across strategy consulting (PwC Strategy&, Deloitte), venture capital (Earlybird VC, Alstin Capital), and startups. I also interned at the United Nations (UNITAR) in Geneva.

In 2024, I moved to Oxford to pursue an MSc in Social Data Science at the Oxford Internet Institute and was part of Keble College. After that, I completed the Le Wagon AI Software Development Bootcamp, building at the intersection of AI, research, and real-world applications.

Beyond academia and tech, I'm passionate about making higher education more accessible and showing that you don't need a conventional background to build an extraordinary career.
