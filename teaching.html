Teaching Philosophy

Great teaching is not only about simplifying. It is about building bridges between what students know and what they are trying to understand. I believe that I only understand a topic, if I am able to explain it to someone else. My teaching has started off as a method for myself to delve deeper into topics and truly understand underlying mechanics. On this path, I found my love for breaking down complex ideas and helping students getting an intuition for these concepts. I focus on making theory tangible, showing real-world application, fostering honest conversations, and creating a space where questions are more valued than answers.
